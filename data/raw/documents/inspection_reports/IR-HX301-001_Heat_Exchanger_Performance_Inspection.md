# IR-HX301-001: Heat Exchanger Performance Inspection

Document ID: IR-HX301-001
Asset ID: HX-301
Asset Name: Feed Preheater Heat Exchanger
Asset Type: Heat Exchanger
Document Type: Inspection Report
Inspection Date: 2026-03-05
Inspector: Demo Inspector
Inspection Status: Completed

---

## 1. Inspection Purpose

This inspection was performed to evaluate the performance condition of HX-301 Feed Preheater Heat Exchanger after reduced outlet temperature was observed during process operation.

The inspection focused on outlet temperature, pressure drop, heat transfer efficiency, fouling symptoms, and cleaning evidence availability.

---

## 2. Inspection Scope

The inspection covered:

* Outlet temperature performance
* Pressure drop across heat exchanger
* Heat transfer efficiency indication
* Cleaning record availability
* Fouling suspicion
* Need for cleaning inspection

---

## 3. Measurement Summary

| Parameter          | Measured Value |  Normal Limit | Warning Limit | Critical Limit | Status  |
| ------------------ | -------------: | ------------: | ------------: | -------------: | ------- |
| Outlet temperature |           88°C | 90°C or above |    Below 90°C |     Below 85°C | Warning |
| Pressure drop      |        1.9 bar | Below 1.2 bar | Above 1.8 bar |  Above 2.2 bar | Warning |
| Efficiency index   |           0.71 |    Above 0.85 |    Below 0.80 |     Below 0.70 | Warning |

---

## 4. Findings

* Outlet temperature was recorded at 88°C, which is below the acceptable target.
* Pressure drop was recorded at 1.9 bar, which is above the warning threshold.
* Heat transfer efficiency index is below the normal range.
* Fouling or restricted flow is suspected.
* Cleaning evidence is overdue for the current inspection cycle.
* Cleaning inspection should be scheduled.

---

## 5. Risk Interpretation

HX-301 is considered medium risk because performance indicators suggest reduced heat transfer efficiency.

The current condition may indicate:

* Tube-side fouling
* Shell-side fouling
* Flow restriction
* Delayed cleaning
* Process-side operating variation

Cleaning evidence must be reviewed because the cleaning record is overdue.

---

## 6. Recommended Action

The maintenance team should:

1. Schedule cleaning inspection.
2. Review last cleaning record.
3. Check outlet temperature trend.
4. Check pressure drop trend.
5. Inspect tube-side condition if accessible.
6. Attach cleaning evidence after maintenance.
7. Review work permit before cleaning activity.

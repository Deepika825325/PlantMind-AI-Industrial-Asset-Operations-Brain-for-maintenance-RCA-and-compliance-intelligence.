# IR-P101-001: Pump Vibration Inspection

Document ID: IR-P101-001
Asset ID: P-101
Asset Name: Cooling Water Circulation Pump
Asset Type: Pump
Document Type: Inspection Report
Inspection Date: 2026-01-21
Inspector: Demo Inspector
Inspection Status: Completed

---

## 1. Inspection Purpose

This inspection was performed to evaluate the vibration condition of P-101 Cooling Water Circulation Pump after abnormal vibration was reported during normal operation.

---

## 2. Inspection Scope

The inspection covered:

* Pump bearing housing vibration
* Visible mounting condition
* Operator-reported vibration behavior
* Need for follow-up bearing inspection
* Need for lubrication verification

---

## 3. Measurement Summary

| Parameter | Measured Value |   Normal Limit | Warning Limit | Critical Limit | Status   |
| --------- | -------------: | -------------: | ------------: | -------------: | -------- |
| Vibration |       7.8 mm/s | Below 4.5 mm/s |      6.0 mm/s |       7.5 mm/s | Critical |

The measured vibration exceeded the critical threshold.

---

## 4. Findings

* Vibration reading was recorded at 7.8 mm/s.
* Critical vibration threshold for P-101 is 7.5 mm/s.
* Abnormal vibration was observed near the bearing housing.
* Operator reported intermittent vibration during normal load.
* Bearing inspection is recommended.
* Shaft alignment check is recommended.
* Lubrication verification is required.

---

## 5. Risk Interpretation

P-101 should be treated as high risk because the vibration value exceeded the critical threshold.

High vibration may indicate:

* Bearing wear
* Lubrication degradation
* Shaft misalignment
* Loose mounting
* Mechanical imbalance

---

## 6. Recommended Action

Create an urgent maintenance work order for:

1. Bearing housing inspection.
2. Lubrication verification.
3. Shaft alignment check.
4. Mounting bolt inspection.
5. Follow-up vibration measurement after corrective action.

## 1. Audit Purpose

This audit was performed to review maintenance and compliance evidence availability for the PlantMind demo assets.

The audit covers:

* P-101 Cooling Water Circulation Pump
* C-201 Process Air Compressor
* HX-301 Feed Preheater Heat Exchanger

The purpose is to identify missing, delayed, or overdue evidence before compliance review.

---

## 2. Audit Scope

The audit reviewed the following evidence categories:

* Vibration inspection evidence
* Lubrication evidence
* Work permit evidence
* Monthly compressor inspection evidence
* Filter replacement verification
* Pressure test evidence
* Cleaning and fouling inspection evidence
* LOTO evidence

## 4. Audit Findings

The audit found five compliance evidence gaps:

1. P-101 weekly lubrication evidence is missing.
2. P-101 work permit evidence is missing before bearing inspection.
3. C-201 filter replacement verification is delayed.
4. HX-301 cleaning and fouling inspection evidence is overdue.
5. HX-301 work permit evidence is missing before cleaning activity.

---

## 5. Risk Interpretation

Missing maintenance evidence can increase operational and compliance risk.

P-101 has both technical risk and compliance risk because high vibration has been detected, but lubrication and work permit evidence are missing.

C-201 has medium compliance risk because filter replacement verification is delayed.

HX-301 has compliance risk because cleaning evidence is overdue and work permit evidence is missing.

---

## 6. Recommended Action

The maintenance and compliance teams should:

1. Upload or complete P-101 lubrication record.
2. Attach work permit evidence for P-101 bearing inspection.
3. Verify C-201 filter replacement completion.
4. Schedule HX-301 cleaning and fouling inspection.
5. Attach work permit evidence before HX-301 cleaning activity.
6. Review all open work orders before final compliance review.

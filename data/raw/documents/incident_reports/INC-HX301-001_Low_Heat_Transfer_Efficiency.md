# INC-HX301-001: Low Heat Transfer Efficiency

Document ID: INC-HX301-001
Asset ID: HX-301
Asset Name: Feed Preheater Heat Exchanger
Asset Type: Heat Exchanger
Document Type: Incident Report
Incident Date: 2026-03-07
Reported By: Operations Team
Incident Status: Open for Maintenance Review

---

## 1. Incident Summary

HX-301 Feed Preheater Heat Exchanger showed reduced heat transfer efficiency during process operation.

The incident was reported after outlet temperature dropped below the acceptable target and pressure drop increased above the warning threshold.

---

## 2. Asset Involved

Asset ID: HX-301
Asset Name: Feed Preheater Heat Exchanger
Asset Type: Shell and Tube Heat Exchanger
Location: Process Area - Unit C
Criticality: Medium

---

## 3. Observed Symptoms

The following symptoms were observed:

* Outlet temperature was below acceptable target.
* Pressure drop was above warning threshold.
* Heat transfer efficiency index was below normal range.
* Cleaning evidence was overdue.
* Fouling or restricted flow was suspected.

---

## 4. Evidence Available

The incident is supported by the following evidence:

| Evidence                                | Source       |
| --------------------------------------- | ------------ |
| Outlet temperature recorded at 88°C     | IR-HX301-001 |
| Pressure drop recorded at 1.9 bar       | IR-HX301-001 |
| Efficiency index recorded at 0.71       | IR-HX301-001 |
| Cleaning evidence overdue               | COMP-001     |
| Cleaning inspection work order required | WO-1005      |

---

## 5. Suspected Causes

Possible causes include:

1. Tube-side fouling
2. Shell-side fouling
3. Flow restriction
4. Delayed cleaning
5. Process-side operating variation

---

## 6. Temporary Action Taken

The operations team continued monitoring HX-301 under controlled operating conditions.

No permanent corrective action was completed at the time of this incident report.

---

## 7. Required Follow-Up

The following follow-up actions are required:

1. Schedule cleaning inspection.
2. Review last cleaning record.
3. Verify outlet temperature trend.
4. Verify pressure drop trend.
5. Inspect tube-side condition if accessible.
6. Attach cleaning evidence after maintenance.
7. Attach work permit evidence before cleaning activity.

---

## 8. Recommended Corrective Action

The maintenance team should schedule cleaning inspection for HX-301 and review fouling indicators.

If fouling is confirmed, cleaning should be completed and cleaning evidence must be attached to the asset record.

---

## 9. RCA Requirement

Root Cause Analysis is recommended because:

* Outlet temperature is below acceptable target.
* Pressure drop is above warning threshold.
* Cleaning evidence is overdue.
* Fouling or restricted flow is suspected.

# INC-P101-001: High Vibration Event

Document ID: INC-P101-001
Asset ID: P-101
Asset Name: Cooling Water Circulation Pump
Asset Type: Pump
Document Type: Incident Report
Incident Date: 2026-01-22
Reported By: Operations Team
Incident Status: Open for RCA

---

## 1. Incident Summary

P-101 Cooling Water Circulation Pump showed repeated high vibration during normal operation.

The incident was reported after vibration readings exceeded the critical threshold and abnormal bearing noise was observed near the bearing housing.

---

## 2. Asset Involved

Asset ID: P-101
Asset Name: Cooling Water Circulation Pump
Asset Type: Centrifugal Pump
Location: Utility Area - Line A
Criticality: High

---

## 3. Observed Symptoms

The following symptoms were observed:

* Vibration exceeded the critical threshold.
* Abnormal bearing noise was observed.
* Bearing temperature was slightly elevated.
* Lubrication evidence was missing for the current week.
* Operator reported intermittent vibration during normal load.

---

## 4. Evidence Available

The incident is supported by the following evidence:

| Evidence                      | Source                  |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Vibration reading of 7.8 mm/s | IR-P101-001             |
| Bearing temperature of 78°C   | IR-P101-002             |
| Missing lubrication evidence  | COMP-001                |
| Abnormal bearing noise        | Maintenance observation |
| Work order required           | WO-1001                 |

---

## 5. Suspected Causes

Possible causes include:

1. Bearing wear
2. Lubrication degradation
3. Shaft misalignment
4. Loose mounting
5. Mechanical imbalance

---

## 6. Temporary Action Taken

The operations team reduced pump load and informed the maintenance team.

No permanent corrective action was completed at the time of this incident report.

---

## 7. Required Follow-Up

The following follow-up actions are required:

1. Generate RCA for P-101 high vibration.
2. Inspect bearing housing.
3. Verify lubrication level and lubricant quality.
4. Check shaft alignment.
5. Inspect mounting bolts.
6. Attach missing lubrication evidence.
7. Attach work permit evidence before physical bearing inspection.

---

## 8. Recommended Corrective Action

The maintenance team should create an urgent maintenance work order for P-101 bearing inspection and lubrication verification.

If bearing damage is confirmed, bearing replacement should be planned during the next safe maintenance window.

---

## 9. RCA Requirement

Root Cause Analysis is required because:

* Vibration exceeded the critical threshold.
* The issue is related to a high-criticality rotating asset.
* Multiple symptoms indicate possible bearing wear.
* Compliance evidence is missing.

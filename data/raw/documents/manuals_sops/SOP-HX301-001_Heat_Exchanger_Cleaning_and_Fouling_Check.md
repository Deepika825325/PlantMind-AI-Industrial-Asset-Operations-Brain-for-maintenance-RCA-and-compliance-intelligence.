---

## 1. Purpose

This SOP defines the cleaning and fouling inspection procedure for HX-301 Feed Preheater Heat Exchanger.

The purpose of this procedure is to detect reduced heat transfer efficiency, identify fouling symptoms, and ensure cleaning evidence is recorded on schedule.

---

## 2. Scope

This SOP applies only to:

Asset ID: HX-301
Asset Name: Feed Preheater Heat Exchanger
Asset Type: Shell and Tube Heat Exchanger
Location: Process Area - Unit C

This SOP does not apply to P-101 Pump or C-201 Compressor.

---

## 3. Fouling Indicators

HX-301 must be reviewed for fouling if any of the following conditions are observed:

* Outlet temperature falls below 90°C.
* Pressure drop exceeds 1.8 bar.
* Heat transfer efficiency decreases.
* Cleaning evidence is overdue.
* Flow restriction is suspected.
* Previous inspection indicates tube-side fouling.

---

## 4. Inspection Frequency

Cleaning and fouling inspection must be performed:

Frequency: Every 30 days
Evidence Required: Yes
Evidence Type: Cleaning record or fouling inspection report

Every cleaning or fouling inspection must have recorded evidence.

---

## 5. Normal Operating Criteria

| Parameter          |   Normal Limit |  Warning Limit | Critical Limit |
| ------------------ | -------------: | -------------: | -------------: |
| Outlet temperature |  90°C or above |     Below 90°C |     Below 85°C |
| Pressure drop      |  Below 1.2 bar |  Above 1.8 bar |  Above 2.2 bar |
| Efficiency index   |     Above 0.85 |     Below 0.80 |     Below 0.70 |
| Cleaning interval  | Within 30 days | Beyond 30 days | Beyond 45 days |

---

## 6. Safety Requirements

Before heat exchanger cleaning or inspection:

1. Obtain maintenance work permit.
2. Isolate heat exchanger from process line.
3. Depressurize equipment before opening.
4. Confirm equipment is drained and cooled.
5. Follow lockout/tagout procedure.
6. Wear required PPE.
7. Confirm safe access before inspection.

---

## 7. Inspection Procedure

1. Review latest outlet temperature trend.
2. Review latest pressure drop trend.
3. Check last cleaning record.
4. Inspect for fouling symptoms.
5. Verify tube-side and shell-side condition if accessible.
6. Record cleaning evidence after completion.
7. Escalate if outlet temperature is below 90°C and pressure drop is above 1.8 bar.

---

## 8. Required Evidence

The following evidence must be maintained:

| Requirement                     | Evidence Required  | Frequency                     |
| ------------------------------- | ------------------ | ----------------------------- |
| Cleaning and fouling inspection | Cleaning record    | Every 30 days                 |
| Pressure drop review            | Inspection report  | During performance inspection |
| Outlet temperature review       | Inspection report  | During performance inspection |
| Work permit                     | Work permit record | Before cleaning               |
| LOTO confirmation               | LOTO record        | Before equipment opening      |

---

## 9. Recommended Action for Abnormal Condition

If HX-301 shows low outlet temperature, high pressure drop, or suspected fouling:

1. Schedule cleaning inspection.
2. Review latest heat exchanger performance inspection.
3. Check pressure drop trend.
4. Check outlet temperature trend.
5. Verify last cleaning record.
6. Attach cleaning evidence after maintenance.
7. Review work permit evidence before cleaning.

## 1. Purpose

This SOP defines the safe shutdown and inspection preparation procedure for C-201 Process Air Compressor.

The purpose of this procedure is to ensure safe compressor isolation before maintenance, inspection, filter verification, or internal checks.

---

## 2. Scope

This SOP applies only to:

Asset ID: C-201
Asset Name: Process Air Compressor
Asset Type: Compressor
Location: Compression Area - Train B

This SOP does not apply to P-101 Pump or HX-301 Heat Exchanger.

---

## 3. Maintenance Triggers

C-201 must be reviewed for maintenance if any of the following conditions are observed:

* Remaining Useful Life trend is decreasing.
* Outlet temperature exceeds 90°C.
* Pressure ratio becomes unstable.
* Filter replacement verification is delayed.
* Abnormal compressor noise is reported.
* Monthly inspection identifies abnormal operating behavior.

---

## 4. Safety Requirements

Before compressor inspection or maintenance:

1. Notify operations team.
2. Obtain maintenance work permit.
3. Reduce compressor load gradually.
4. Isolate compressor from process line.
5. Depressurize compressor section.
6. Verify temperature and pressure stabilization.
7. Apply lockout/tagout.
8. Confirm zero-energy state before maintenance.

---

## 5. Normal Operating Criteria

| Parameter          |    Normal Range |        Warning Limit |            Critical Limit |
| ------------------ | --------------: | -------------------: | ------------------------: |
| Outlet temperature |    70°C to 85°C |           Above 90°C |                Above 95°C |
| Pressure ratio     |      3.2 to 3.8 |            Above 3.9 |                 Above 4.1 |
| Estimated RUL      | Above 50 cycles |      Below 40 cycles |           Below 25 cycles |
| Filter condition   |        Verified | Verification delayed | Filter blockage suspected |

---

## 6. Shutdown Procedure

1. Inform control room before shutdown.
2. Reduce compressor load gradually.
3. Stop compressor according to operating procedure.
4. Isolate inlet and outlet lines.
5. Depressurize compressor section.
6. Confirm pressure is within safe range.
7. Allow equipment temperature to stabilize.
8. Apply LOTO.
9. Record shutdown confirmation.

---

## 7. Inspection Preparation

After safe shutdown:

1. Review latest RUL trend.
2. Review outlet temperature trend.
3. Check pressure ratio history.
4. Verify filter replacement record.
5. Inspect compressor stage if degradation trend is present.
6. Create or update maintenance work order.
7. Attach inspection evidence.

---

## 8. Required Evidence

The following evidence must be maintained:

| Requirement                     | Evidence Required             | Frequency                   |
| ------------------------------- | ----------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| Monthly compressor inspection   | Inspection report             | Monthly                     |
| Filter replacement verification | Maintenance log or work order | As scheduled                |
| RUL trend review                | RUL report                    | During maintenance planning |
| Safe shutdown confirmation      | Shutdown checklist            | Before inspection           |
| Work permit                     | Work permit record            | Before maintenance          |
| LOTO confirmation               | LOTO record                   | Before equipment opening    |

---

## 9. Recommended Action for Abnormal Condition

If C-201 shows decreasing RUL, high outlet temperature, or pressure instability:

1. Schedule compressor inspection during the next maintenance window.
2. Verify filter replacement completion.
3. Review compressor operating conditions.
4. Check outlet temperature trend.
5. Check pressure ratio trend.
6. Review RUL report before maintenance planning.

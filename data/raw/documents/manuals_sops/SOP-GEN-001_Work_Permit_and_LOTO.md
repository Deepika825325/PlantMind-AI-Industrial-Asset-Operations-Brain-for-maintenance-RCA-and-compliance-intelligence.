## 1. Purpose

This SOP defines the general work permit and lockout tagout requirements for maintenance activities performed on PlantMind demo assets.

The purpose of this procedure is to ensure that maintenance work is controlled, documented, and performed only after required safety evidence is available.

---

## 2. Scope

This SOP applies to maintenance work involving:

* P-101 Cooling Water Circulation Pump
* C-201 Process Air Compressor
* HX-301 Feed Preheater Heat Exchanger

This SOP applies to inspection, cleaning, equipment opening, bearing inspection, compressor inspection, pressure-related maintenance, and isolation activities.

---

## 3. Work Permit Requirement

A maintenance work permit is required before:

1. Opening pump bearing housing.
2. Performing compressor shutdown and inspection.
3. Cleaning or opening heat exchanger sections.
4. Performing pressure-related inspection.
5. Performing maintenance that requires equipment isolation.
6. Performing work that may expose personnel to mechanical, electrical, pressure, or thermal hazards.

---

## 4. Lockout Tagout Requirement

Lockout tagout is required before:

1. Physical inspection of P-101 bearing housing.
2. Maintenance on C-201 compressor.
3. Cleaning or opening HX-301 heat exchanger.
4. Electrical isolation.
5. Mechanical isolation.
6. Depressurization and equipment opening.

---

## 5. Required Evidence

The following evidence must be recorded before maintenance work begins:

| Requirement           | Evidence Required                                   |
| --------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Work permit           | Work permit number and approval                     |
| LOTO confirmation     | Lockout tagout checklist                            |
| Asset identification  | Asset ID and asset name                             |
| Maintenance reason    | Inspection, cleaning, fault, or planned maintenance |
| Supervisor approval   | Name and timestamp                                  |
| Technician assignment | Technician name                                     |
| Safety confirmation   | Isolation and zero-energy confirmation              |

---

## 6. Minimum Evidence Fields

Every work permit or LOTO evidence record must include:

* Asset ID
* Work order ID
* Permit number
* Date
* Technician name
* Supervisor name
* Isolation confirmation
* LOTO confirmation
* Work description
* Approval status

---

## 7. Asset-Specific Safety Notes

### P-101 Pump

For P-101 bearing inspection:

* Work permit is required before opening bearing housing.
* LOTO is required before physical inspection.
* Lubrication evidence must be reviewed before maintenance.
* Vibration inspection evidence should be reviewed before corrective action.

### C-201 Compressor

For C-201 compressor inspection:

* Compressor must be safely shut down.
* System must be depressurized.
* Outlet temperature and pressure must stabilize.
* Filter replacement verification should be reviewed.

### HX-301 Heat Exchanger

For HX-301 cleaning inspection:

* Equipment must be isolated.
* Equipment must be depressurized.
* Cleaning work requires work permit evidence.
* Cleaning evidence must be recorded after maintenance.

---

## 8. Compliance Review

A maintenance activity is considered non-compliant if:

* Work permit evidence is missing.
* LOTO evidence is missing.
* Required inspection evidence is missing.
* Required cleaning evidence is overdue.
* Filter replacement verification is delayed.
* Lubrication evidence is missing.

## 1. Purpose

This procedure defines the weekly lubrication and bearing inspection requirements for P-101 Cooling Water Circulation Pump.

The purpose of this SOP is to ensure that the pump bearing system remains properly lubricated, abnormal bearing noise is detected early, and vibration-related failures are prevented.
